I picked up my first record at target, only because I liked the cover art.

I diplayed it on my wall and after a few weeks I got another to go next to it.

Eventually, I had collected about 10, when I thought I should probably have

a record player so I could actually listen to them.

This was all over a year ago, and today I have over 30 vinyl records,

and a little corner in my room with a turn table and speakers to listen to them.

One of my favoite things to do is stop in at a record store and see what gems I can find.
