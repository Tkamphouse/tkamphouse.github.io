Biking

During the covid quarantine, I took up road biking so I could do

something to get me out of the house. I would bike all around Sumas

eventually to the point where I had biked every road in the town,

which isn't that hard, it is a tiny town. One day I was so tired of

biking the same old roads I through my bike over the border and started biking there.

Needless to say, I didn't get away with it.

More recently I have gotten into mountain biking. It's definitely a lot

more intense than road biking, but nothing beats the adrenaline and being

surrounded by nature while hurling down a mountain.
